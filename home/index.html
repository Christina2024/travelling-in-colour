Solo travel 101

The thought of solo-travelling can be overwhelming for most. However, what it has shown me is a new form of reconnecting with myself and connecting with the communities I travel to. Travelling alone as a woman, I recommend starting out small first! This looks like:
  • Taking yourself out to eat alone
  • Visiting surrounding domestic cities by yourself
  • Going to the cinemas by yourself
    • to different states alone. I used to do this all the time before making my first international solo trip, and it allowed me to build up confidence in navigating the world alone.

    You can never be too prepared when traveling alone. Be sure to conduct thorough research about the places you visit. This includes crime rates, weather, other solo travel experiences from the area, what to do, nightlife, and basically everything you can think of. I recommend watching YouTube videos to get an authentic feel for a destination you will be seeing or want to see in the future.
